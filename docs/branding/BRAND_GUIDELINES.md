# Brand Guidelines - MugnaDigital

## 1. Brand Overview
MugnaDigital represents the intersection of creation ("Mugna") and technology. Our brand identity is minimalist, clean, and modern, reflecting a high-performance tech startup aesthetic.

## 2. Color Palette

### Primary Colors
These colors form the foundation of our interface and brand materials.

- **Midnight Black** (Backgrounds, Dark Themes)
  - Hex: `#09090b`
  - Tailwind: `zinc-950`
  - RGB: `9, 9, 11`

- **Pure White** (Text on Dark, Cards on Light)
  - Hex: `#ffffff`
  - Tailwind: `white`
  - RGB: `255, 255, 255`

### Secondary Colors
Used for subtle contrasts, borders, and secondary text.

- **Slate Gray** (Secondary Text)
  - Hex: `#a1a1aa`
  - Tailwind: `zinc-400`
  - RGB: `161, 161, 170`

- **Subtle Wash** (Light Backgrounds)
  - Hex: `#f4f4f5`
  - Tailwind: `zinc-100`
  - RGB: `244, 244, 245`

### Accent Color
Used sparingly for Calls to Action (CTAs), links, and key highlights.

- **Electric Blue**
  - Hex: `#2563eb`
  - Tailwind: `blue-600`
  - RGB: `37, 99, 235`

## 3. Typography

**Primary Font:** `Inter`
A clean, modern sans-serif typeface that offers excellent readability at all sizes.

- **Headings:** Bold (700) or Semibold (600). Tight letter spacing (-0.02em).
- **Body:** Regular (400) or Medium (500). Normal letter spacing.
- **Monospace:** `Geist Mono` or `Fira Code` for code snippets.

## 4. Logo Guidelines

### Wordmark
The primary logo is a text-based wordmark: **MugnaDigital**.

- **Font:** Inter Bold.
- **Style:** Minimalist.
- **Color:** White on dark backgrounds, Black on light backgrounds.
- **Variations:**
  - Full: `MugnaDigital`
  - Short: `MD` (Icon)

### Usage
- Ensure ample whitespace (padding) around the logo.
- Do not distort, stretch, or apply heavy shadows.
- Keep the logo horizontal.

## 5. Imagery
- **Style:** Abstract, geometric, or high-quality product screenshots.
- **Vibe:** Tech-focused, futuristic but grounded.
- **Treatment:** Use grayscale or duotone effects with the brand's Deep Blue/Black tint.
