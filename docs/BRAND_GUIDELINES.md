# Brand Guidelines - MugnaDigital

## 1. Logo
- **Concept:** A simplified text-based logo representing code/tech.
- **Symbol:** `{M}`
- **Usage:**
  - Favicon: `{M}`
  - Header: `MugnaDigital` (Text)

## 2. Typography
- **Headings (Display):** Space Grotesk
  - Usage: H1, H2, H3, Hero text.
  - Variable: `--font-display` / `font-display`
- **Body:** Inter
  - Usage: Paragraphs, UI elements, buttons.
  - Variable: `--font-sans` / `font-sans`

## 3. Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Blue / Black (`#171717` or similar dark)
- **Secondary:** White / Light Gray
- **Accent:** Electric Blue / Emerald Green

## 4. Tone of Voice
**"Professional but Senpai-like"**

We are experienced, knowledgeable, and helpful. We guide our clients with confidence but without arrogance.

- **Encouraging:** "You're on the right track. Let's make it scalable."
- **Clear:** Avoid unnecessary jargon. Explain complex tech in simple terms.
- **Mentorship:** We don't just build; we empower.
- **Direct:** We value your time.

### Examples:
- **Instead of:** "We leverage synergistic paradigms to optimize your ROI."
- **Say:** "We build systems that grow with your business and save you money."

- **Instead of:** "Error 404."
- **Say:** "Looks like you wandered off the map. Let's get you back home."
